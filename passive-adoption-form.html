Passive Adoption Terms

Thanks for taking a gentle step toward passive adoption. This form helps us understand the kind of home and companionship you are open to, so we can guide a thoughtful match.

How this works:

We review every response with care and may contact you if we see a match that feels right.

Submission does not guarantee immediate placement or availability of a specific dog.

We may reach out to learn a little more about your routine, space, and preferences.

What we ask of you:

Please share accurate details so we can keep the matching process honest and helpful.

By submitting, you agree to stay in touch if our team reaches out with a possible match.
