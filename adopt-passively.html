Terms & Conditions

Dear Adopter, Thank you for choosing to embark on the journey of pet adoption with Safescape Foundation. We appreciate your commitment to providing a loving home for a furry companion. Our goal is to facilitate the perfect match between pets and adopters, taking into consideration the unique characteristics of both the animal and your living situation.

As part of our adoption process, we utilize this application as an initial step to align your lifestyle, needs, and experience with our knowledge of the animals in our care. Rest assured, we are dedicated to ensuring that each animal finds a suitable and enduring home.

For your awareness, kindly note the following important information:

  1. Financial Responsibility: All adopters are accountable for veterinary care and medical expenses incurred post-adoption.
  2. Re-homing Procedures: In the event that circumstances necessitate re-homing your new pet, we kindly request that you contact us first.
  3. Verification of Information: We reserve the right to verify all information provided on the adoption application, including veterinary references and landlord details.

By affixing your signature below, you acknowledge the following:

  • I certify that the information provided in this application is accurate. Any misrepresentation may result in the forfeiture of the privilege to adopt a pet.
  • I understand that the adoption process is not on a first-come, first-serve basis, and Safescape Foundation reserves the right to deny adoption requests.
  • I authorize the investigation of all statements contained in this application.

Thank you for your prompt attention to these matters. All responses and information shared will be treated with the utmost confidentiality.

Please be advised that due to the high volume of applications, only successful applicants will be contacted within 1-3 business days.

Sincerely,
