Safescape Foundation

Privacy Policy

Safescape Foundation is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our website, services, and programs. This page mirrors the policy content currently published on the live Safescape site.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information in a few different ways.

  • Directly from you when you submit an adoption application.
  • When you fill out a pet surrender form.
  • When you apply to become a foster parent.
  • When you express interest in volunteering.
  • When you make a donation.
  • When you create an account on the website, if applicable.
  • When you sign up for newsletters or other communications.
  • Automatically through device and browsing information such as IP address, browser type, and pages accessed.

Types of Information

  • Personal details including name, email, phone number, mailing address, ID details, and payment information for donations.
  • Demographic information including age, location, and household composition.
  • Animal-related details including species, breed, age, medical history, and temperament for surrendered or adopted animals.
  • Device and browsing activity data such as IP address, browser type, cookies, and accessed pages.

How Safescape Uses Your Information

  • Process adoption applications and match pets with suitable homes.
  • Follow up with adopters, foster parents, volunteers, and donors.
  • Send updates about Safescape’s work, events, and campaigns.
  • Respond to inquiries and requests.
  • Process donations and manage accounts where applicable.
  • Verify information shared in applications and forms.
  • Analyze website traffic and improve the user experience.
  • Conduct fundraising campaigns.

Sharing Your Information

Safescape does not share personal information with third parties without consent, except in the following situations:

  • As required by law or legal process.
  • To protect the safety of others.
  • To investigate fraudulent or abusive activity.
  • To trusted service providers assisting with operations such as website hosting and payment processing, and only with the information necessary for them to perform those services.

Security, Cookies, and Your Choices

  • Safescape uses security measures including secure servers with encryption, access controls, and regular backups.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies may be used to personalize your experience and analyze traffic.
  • You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • You may request access to, updates to, or deletion of your personal information.
  • You may also manage your communication subscription preferences.

Children’s Privacy and Policy Changes

  • Safescape’s website and services are not intended for children under 13.
  • Safescape does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • This policy may be updated from time to time, with revised versions posted on the website.
